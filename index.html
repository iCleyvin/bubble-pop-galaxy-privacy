Privacy Policy

Bubble Pop Galaxy
Last updated: March 30, 2026

Information We Collect

Bubble Pop Galaxy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (scores, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob - Displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing - Processes in-app purchases. Purchase history is managed by Google Play. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for a general audience (ages 13+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Storage

Game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage. No data is transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

This app displays banner ads, interstitial ads, and optional rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. You can remove banner and interstitial ads through an in-app purchase. Rewarded ads are always optional and user-initiated.

In-App Purchases

This app offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. All transactions are handled by Google Play's secure billing system.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
